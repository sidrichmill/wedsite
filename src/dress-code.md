# Dress Code

**Garden Formal.** Think elevated, but comfortable enough for grass underfoot — the ceremony and reception are both outdoors.

<div class="panel">
  <h2>Suggested palette</h2>
  <p>We'd love to see soft greens, warm terracottas, and dusty blues — but please don't feel boxed in. Wear what makes you feel great.</p>
</div>

## For most guests
Cocktail dresses, jumpsuits, or a suit with or without a tie. Flat or low-heeled shoes are a good idea — part of the evening is on lawn.

## What to skip
All-white or ivory (reserved for the couple), and jeans or overly casual wear.

Questions about what to wear? Reach out any time — we're happy to help.
